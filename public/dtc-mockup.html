BYD Atto 3 — B110A High Voltage Battery Coolant Pump Fault

System: High Voltage Battery Thermal
12
Cases logged
3
Causes identified

The high voltage battery cooling pump has reported an abnormal condition. This fault indicates the battery thermal management system may not be operating at full capacity. In warm ambient conditions, battery performance and range may be reduced until the pump issue is resolved.

Drive advisory

In most cases, yes — but schedule a service appointment soon. Monitor the warning closely. If additional symptoms appear (loss of power, unusual noises), pull over safely and contact your dealer.

01 Likely Causes · BYD Atto 3
  • 01Coolant pump motor winding failure due to prolonged operation in high ambient temperature (common in QLD/NT)
  • 02Coolant pump connector corrosion or loose harness pin — intermittent contact loss triggers the fault
  • 03Low coolant level causing pump cavitation and current spike, misidentified as pump fault by BMS
02 What To Do
  • 01
    Clear the fault and monitor
    Use a BYD-compatible OBD tool to clear the code. If it returns within 50km, escalate to a dealer immediately.
  • 02
    Check coolant level and condition
    Locate the coolant reservoir under the bonnet. Low level or discolouration indicates a leak or degraded fluid — both require dealer attention.
  • 03
    Book a BYD dealer inspection
    Request a coolant pump functional test. Pump replacement runs approximately A$450–750 parts + labour under non-warranty conditions.
03 Real-World Cases
Reddit r/BYDAtto3 · Brisbane, QLD · Feb 2025

B110A appeared after a long summer drive. Car showed reduced charging speed. Dealer found coolant pump drawing abnormal current. Replaced under warranty.

Coolant pump replaced under warranty. No recurrence in 3 months.
AusEV Forums · Sydney, NSW · Dec 2024

Fault appeared twice in one week, cleared itself both times. Found connector on pump harness was loose — reseated and secured with electrical tape. No further faults.

Harness connector re-seated. Fixed without parts replacement. Cost: nil.
